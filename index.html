Introduction

Grade 9 Personality Test:
Every student is unique. Some are quiet and thoughtful, others are outgoing and energetic. Understanding personality helps with learning, teamwork, and career planning. This test provides students and parents with a clear view of natural tendencies, strengths, and suitable learning styles. It is short, easy to understand, and results can be downloaded. Inspired by Four Temperaments and MBTI, adapted for Thai students.

Grade 9 Aptitude Test:
This test helps students discover natural strengths in practical skills, creativity, problem-solving, social helping, or leadership. Designed for Thai students, it reflects their school, home, and social environments. Results are reliable, easy to interpret, and downloadable. Adapted from Howard Gardner’s Multiple Intelligences, simplified for Thai students.
